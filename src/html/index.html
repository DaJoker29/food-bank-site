Equipment Restoration

and Reutilization

Our focus from the beginning is to reduce the equipment costs for nonprofit organizations. At the same time, we're making sure our donors receive the most out of their tax write off. With the price of scrap steel at pennies per pound, weighed to the cost of preparation and processing, the "profit" made from scrapping isn't worth the effort.

This program creates a viable alternative that provides companies with a great financial benefit and provides equipment to organizations in need.

What we offer is a single solution to removing your equipment as-is, at no cost to you or your company. Your company will receive a tax deduction at a fair rate, well above scrap prices, that in turn can save your business money on its taxes.

On our end equipment is evaluated and restored to proper working condition. We have a stable of volunteer mechanics and forklift repair shops who help in this process. From this point, a non profit agency can apply for the equipment that has been refurbished. We evaluate their particular need and provide the necessary equipment in order for them to continue functioning properly. These nonprofit agencies must be certified with the state of California and must sign a contract stating that the equipment if for nonprofit use only and can be returned to the refurbishment program if no longer needed. This equipment is on loan to them as long as they need it. They may not sell it or give it to another organization. Along with the contract there is a deposit to ensure that the equipment will not be sold and the deposit will be refunded upon the return of the equipment in fully working order.

His R.A.F.T. Inc. has over 32 years of providing help across the state and this is just the next step for us to help provide for even the smallest nonprofit organization.
