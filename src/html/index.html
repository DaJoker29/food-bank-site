His R.A.F.T. is a Central Valley nonprofit corporation that has been serving the community for over 30 years. Our mission is to provide those in our area with the means to serve the underfed.

We operate as a bulk distributor to other nonprofit organizations. We do not give directly to the public. We also accept many types of donations from businesses. All donations are tax-deductible.
